About us

At Pursue Career we are all former athletes ourselves, and know first hand the skills and attributes that we develop competing in elite sport.

Kim Moe Krohn

Kim Moe Krohn from Oslo, Norway is an entrepreneur and managing director of the Pursue Group. He played full-time soccer on a scholarship for the University of South Carolina Upstate while earning his bachelor's degree in communication.

MORE ABOUT KIM

Stewart Stanbra

Stewart Stanbra from Evesham, England, is an entrepreneur and head of business development for the Pursue Group. He played full-time soccer on a scholarship for the University of South Carolina Upstate and Canisius College while obtaining a bachelor's degree.

MORE ABOUT KIM

Stewart and Kim founded College Scholarships USA Ltd. (www.csusa.no) which has helped 1500+ student-athletes to obtain scholarships to prestigious American Universities while competing in elite sports.

Our network and track record with Pursue Pro and CSUSA give us direct access and long term relationships with thousands of student-athletes we have worked with since 2007.
